Places I have visited


🇦🇪 UAE , Dubai

From experience, the UAE is a unique mix of tradition and modern life. It’s safe, vibrant, and full of opportunities. The heat can be tough, but the lifestyle and energy make it worth it.

There’s always something new to explore, from futuristic buildings to cultural events. It's a place that really grows on you over time.

🇫🇷 France , Paris

Paris is a beautiful mix of history, art, and everyday charm. It’s elegant, full of culture, and always inspiring. The crowds can be a lot, but the atmosphere makes up for it.

There’s always something to discover, from cozy cafés to world-famous landmarks. It’s a city that stays with you long after you leave.

🇪🇸 Spain , Barcelona

colorful, energetic, and full of life — from Gaudí’s art to beachside sunsets. The vibe is relaxed yet creative, and food lovers will never get bored.

🇲🇰 Macedonia , Skopje

has a unique charm with its mix of old and new, quirky statues, and mountain views. It may feel unusual at first, but it grows on you quickly.

🇷🇸 Serbia , Belgrade

lively, and full of personality. Its nightlife, rivers, and friendly locals make it unforgettable in its own bold way.
